What we do

Our service is Unique .

Mission Statement

Our challenge is to provide a well segmented healthcare option and services. Our mission is to develop and create a portable hospital which will provide any type of services and help a person gets from hospital.

Our Solution

We will introduce an web-application which has a feature to book an appointment through a dedicated portal from the application UI .Our medical team will reach the patient within 15-30 minutes . He will be examined by the doctors and will be given immediate treatment which can save his/her life .

Business Concept

Our portable hospital is specially for the persons in rural areas and the elder persons whose condition is not so much serious to admit in a hospital but need instant treatment . From our application or website user can give us a message/call and we will reach the patient within 10-15 minutes .
